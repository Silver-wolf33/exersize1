CoU Pitha Ghar

Bengali pitha is a traditional rice-based delicacy of Bengal, mostly enjoyed during winter festivals.
Popular varieties include Pati Shapta, Bhapa Pitha, and Chitoi Pitha, made with rice flour, coconut, and jaggery. Pitha represents the warmth, tradition, and cultural richness of Bengali cuisine.
